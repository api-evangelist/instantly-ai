The article evaluates leading B2B contact discovery platforms based on data accuracy, deliverability features, and true cost of ownership. It emphasizes that contact data decays annually and recommends combining verified databases with email warmup capabilities to protect sender reputation. The analysis compares five major tools -- Sales Navigator, Apollo, ZoomInfo, Hunter, and RocketReach -- concluding that flat-fee models outperform per-seat pricing for scaling teams, and integrated deliverability infrastructure is essential for ensuring messages reach primary inboxes rather than spam folders.

This guide teaches B2B sales teams to combine LinkedIn Sales Navigator's advanced filters with Boolean search strings to identify decision-makers and full buying committees at scale. The article emphasizes that LinkedIn remains the most current professional database because members self-report their data, and it provides a repeatable prospecting system using function-based filtering, seniority levels, and buying signals like recent job changes or funding rounds. Critical to the process is verifying all contact email addresses before launching campaigns and warming sending accounts to maintain sender reputation and keep bounce rates below 1%.
